About Me

Creativity drives my life. I am pursuing the field of UX UI designing because I enjoy the whole journey of ideation, research, the technical process and the final outcome. The act of creating itself gives me a sense of satisfaction. I believe that there is always room for improvement and this motivates me to keep learning. I am currently working at CityMall and have done internships at NeenOpal, Pinky Promise, Fluidesigns and freelance for Content Turbine and WorkDuck . I have completed my dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) from IIT Kharagpur.
